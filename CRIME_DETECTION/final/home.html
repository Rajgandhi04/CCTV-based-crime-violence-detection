Video Analytics Explained

Video analytics is a technology that leverages video surveillance to extract useful information from video footage. It uses advanced algorithms and machine learning techniques to analyze video data and provide insights into various aspects of the video content.

How Video Analytics Works

Video analytics works by analyzing video data in real-time or after the fact. It can detect and track objects, recognize faces, identify license plates, and more. It can also be used to monitor traffic, detect anomalies, and provide situational awareness in various settings.
